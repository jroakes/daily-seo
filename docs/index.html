Digital Decay Threatens Web Content Accessibility

A new study by Pew Research Center reveals the alarming extent of "digital decay," with a significant percentage of web pages from previous years now inaccessible. The study found that 38% of web pages from 2013 are unavailable today, highlighting the transient nature of online information. This decay also affects news and government websites, with a high prevalence of broken links.
